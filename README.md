# 📊 Project Overview

This project delivers a comprehensive analytics solution to evaluate **Adidas US Sales Performance ($899.90M Total Revenue)** across multiple regions, retailers, product categories, and sales channels.

Using **Tableau Public** and **Microsoft Excel**, the dashboard transforms raw sales data into actionable business intelligence, helping stakeholders identify profitability drivers, regional trends, retailer performance, and revenue growth opportunities.

---

# 🎯 Business Objective

✔ Analyze overall sales performance

✔ Identify high-profit product categories

✔ Evaluate retailer contribution

✔ Compare sales channel effectiveness

✔ Discover regional growth opportunities

✔ Support data-driven business decisions
