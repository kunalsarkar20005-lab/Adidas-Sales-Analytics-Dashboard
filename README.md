## 🎯 Executive Summary & Objective
This project delivers a comprehensive data analytics solution to evaluate **Adidas' US sales performance ($899.90M Total Revenue)**. The objective is to identify key profitability drivers, optimize channel efficiency, analyze regional trends, and assess retailer performance to enable data-driven business strategy and inventory optimization.

---

## 💡 Key Business Insights Discovered

### 1. High-Level Financial Performance
| Metric | Value | Business Impact |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Total Sales** | $899.90M | Strong market presence across the US. |
| **Operating Profit** | $332.13M | Healthy bottom-line performance. |
| **Total Units Sold** | 2.48M | High volume distribution across product lines. |
| **Avg. Price Per Unit** | $45.22 | Baseline pricing benchmark for future forecasting. |

### 2. Product Profitability Optimization (Operating Margin)
* **Top Performers:** `Men's Street Footwear` (**44.61%**) and `Women's Apparel` (**44.13%**) yield the highest operating margins, marking them as high-priority focus areas for marketing.
* **Volume Drivers:** While margins vary, product lines like Men's Street Footwear also lead in absolute units sold (5.93M scaled benchmark).

### 3. Retailer Distribution Efficiency
* **Strategic Partnerships:** `West Gear ($242.96M)` and `Foot Locker ($220.09M)` emerge as primary revenue generators, driving over 50% of the total distribution.
* **Underperforming Channels:** `Walmart ($74.56M)` shows the lowest sales volume, indicating a need for contract renegotiation or promotional optimization.

### 4. Sales Method Performance (Omnichannel Insights)
* **In-Store:** Dominates the market with **$356.64M**, proving physical retail is still the strongest channel.
* **Outlet & Online:** Outlet sales account for **$295.59M**, while Online channels generate **$247.67M**, indicating a robust digital presence that can be further scaled.

### 5. Geographical & Regional Dominance
* **Top Region:** The **West Region ($89.61M Operating Profit)** is the most profitable territory.
* **Top States (Revenue):** **New York ($64.23M)** and **California ($60.17M)** are the core state-level markets driving maximum volume.
