# 📖 Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive analysis of Adidas sales performance across the United States using Tableau and Microsoft Excel.

The dashboard is designed to help business stakeholders understand sales trends, profitability drivers, retailer contributions, regional performance, and customer purchasing behavior through interactive visual analytics.

The objective is to transform raw transactional data into meaningful business insights that support strategic decision-making and revenue optimization.

---

# 🎯 Business Objective

Adidas operates across multiple:

- Regions
- States
- Retailers
- Product Categories
- Sales Channels

Understanding which business segments contribute the most revenue and profit is essential for making informed strategic decisions.

This dashboard helps answer critical business questions:

✔ Which products generate the highest profitability?

✔ Which retailers contribute most to revenue?

✔ Which regions outperform others?

✔ How effective are Online, Outlet, and In-Store sales?

✔ Where should future investments be focused?

# 🔥 Key Business Insights

---

## 👟 Product Performance Analysis

### Highest Operating Margin Categories

| Product Category | Operating Margin |
|-----------------|-----------------:|
| Men's Street Footwear | 44.61% |
| Women's Apparel | 44.13% |

### Business Insight

These categories generate the highest profitability and should be prioritized for:

- Marketing Campaigns
- Inventory Planning
- Product Expansion Strategies
- Revenue Growth Initiatives

---

## 🏪 Retailer Performance Analysis

| Retailer | Revenue Generated |
|----------|-----------------:|
| West Gear | $242.96M |
| Foot Locker | $220.09M |
| Walmart | $74.56M |

### Business Insight

West Gear and Foot Locker collectively account for a significant portion of total revenue, making them critical strategic retail partners.

---

## 🛒 Sales Channel Performance

| Sales Method | Revenue |
|-------------|---------:|
| In-Store | $356.64M |
| Outlet | $295.59M |
| Online | $247.67M |

### Business Insight

Physical retail stores continue to dominate revenue generation while online sales demonstrate strong future growth potential.

---

## 🌎 Regional Performance Analysis

### Most Profitable Region

🏆 West Region

**Operating Profit:** $89.61M

---

### Top Revenue States

🥇 New York — $64.23M

🥈 California — $60.17M

### Business Insight

The West Region remains the strongest profit contributor and should continue receiving strategic focus and investment.
