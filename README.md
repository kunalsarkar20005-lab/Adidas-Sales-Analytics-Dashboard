# 📖 Project Overview

This project delivers a comprehensive analytics solution to evaluate **Adidas US Sales Performance** across multiple regions, retailers, product categories, and sales channels.

Using **Tableau Public** and **Microsoft Excel**, the dashboard transforms raw sales records into meaningful business intelligence that supports strategic decision-making, profitability analysis, and revenue optimization.

---

# 🎯 Project Objective

The primary objective of this project is to:

- Analyze overall sales performance.
- Identify top-performing products.
- Evaluate retailer contribution.
- Compare sales channels.
- Discover regional trends.
- Measure profitability across business segments.
- Support data-driven decision making.

# 🔥 Key Business Insights

## 👟 Product Performance Analysis

| Product Category | Operating Margin |
|------------------|-----------------:|
| Men's Street Footwear | 44.61% |
| Women's Apparel | 44.13% |

### Key Finding

- Men's Street Footwear generates the highest operating margin.
- Women's Apparel closely follows as a major profit contributor.
- These categories represent the strongest opportunities for future growth and marketing investment.

---

## 🏪 Retailer Performance Analysis

| Retailer | Revenue |
|----------|---------:|
| West Gear | $242.96M |
| Foot Locker | $220.09M |
| Walmart | $74.56M |

### Key Finding

- West Gear is the largest revenue-generating retailer.
- Foot Locker contributes significantly to total sales volume.
- Walmart records the lowest sales among major retail partners.

---

## 🛒 Sales Channel Analysis

| Sales Method | Revenue |
|--------------|---------:|
| In-Store | $356.64M |
| Outlet | $295.59M |
| Online | $247.67M |

### Key Finding

- In-Store sales remain the dominant channel.
- Outlet stores continue to perform strongly.
- Online sales show significant potential for future expansion.

---

## 🌎 Regional Performance Analysis

### 🏆 Most Profitable Region

**West Region**

Operating Profit: **$89.61M**

### 🥇 Top Revenue States

| State | Revenue |
|--------|---------:|
| New York | $64.23M |
| California | $60.17M |

### Key Finding

- The West Region is the strongest contributor to profitability.
- New York and California lead overall revenue generation.
